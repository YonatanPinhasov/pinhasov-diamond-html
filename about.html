About Me

I was Born in Israel. When i turned 1 years old my parents took me to the USA. I lived in the states for most of my life. I had a pretty nice life there, I met good people. When i turned 13 my parents got divorced. I ended up living with my mom and we moved to Arizona.

In Arizona I went to junior high school. After a year there my mom sent me back to my dad. Me and my dad moved to Israel trying to make things good. After 2 years I moved back to the states. In the states I worked in the diamond bussiness.

2 Years later i moved back to Israel alone to make my own life. At 18 i joined the army in "Black Cats unit". I spent around 3 in a half years in the army finding my place. I ended up finding my wife there and still looking for myself. After leaving the army life was hard.

I worked in so many jobs a driver, warehouse sorter and even in HVAC. Now im studying FullStack Web Dev and I think I finaly found my calling.
